## CONVERSION EXPECTATIONS

Based on industry data for beta insurtech products:
- **Cold email open rate:** 15-25% (with good subject lines)
- **Reply rate:** 5-10%
- **Demo conversion:** 30-50% of replies
- **Pilot signup from demo:** 40-60%
- **Overall conversion (email → pilot):** 2-5%
- **Pilot → paying customer:** 20-30%

**To get 10-15 paying beta customers:**
- Send 100 targeted emails → 15-25 opens → 5-10 replies → 3-5 demos → 2-3 pilot signups
- Repeat 3-4 rounds of 100 emails
- Supplement with LinkedIn outreach and conference networking
